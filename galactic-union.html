UNIFICATION IN SPACE TO EXPLORE

WE must create a galactic FEDERATION where all people are united to explore the mysterious infinite void of space.

One of the reasons that the United Nations is not performing as well as it should be is its focus on individual nations. They do not look beyond national borders much. We should be focusing not just on currently making peace between existing nations on Earth, but on completely uniting humanity as one, because when humanity truly unites as one, it's an amazing species. The UN's goal is to "end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity." This pretty much says it all. Evidently, they don't look beyond national borders much, and they only focus on Earth. They only want existing divided nations to be at some level of peace. That is not good. Humanity needs leaders, organizations, and companies that look beyond national borders. Otherwise, we're toast. Additionally, if the UN stays in charge, we'll basically be focusing on "world peace" forever. World Peace for what? If we are to achieve world peace at all, it should be just the first step in uniting humanity to eventually explore the stars as a species together. The United Nations' entire mission is to only achieve what we believe is the first step in uniting humanity. That's a horrible long-term goal.

According to NBC News, humans have already made contact with a "galactic federation" founded by aliens, which are waiting for humanity to reach a stage where it understands what space and spaceships are, before revealing themselves to us. "The Unidentified Flying Objects have asked not to publish that they are here, humanity is not ready yet," says Haim Eshed, former head of Israel's Defense Ministry's space directorate. Eshed added that US President Donald Trump was aware of the extraterrestrials' existence and had been "on the verge of revealing" information but was asked not to in order to prevent "mass hysteria." Though everything described in that report would make perfect sense and would be logical, we, the general public, have no direct proof of them existing, and because of that, we must assume that they don't exist, and must build a galactic federation ourselves. Just imagine a time where humanity is united, free of worry, thriving in the depths of space. We likely eventually will, but, if we learn to work together faster, space news websites in the year 2036 could look like this (click). We don't want to keep waking up everyday thinking about how terrible the world is. That's a great way to live a sad and depressing life. We want to wake up and think about how great life is and how exciting our future living among the stars will be. This is why we should learn to work together ASAP.
