To the stars through difficulties

Atmos is a nongovernmental organization based in the United States, focused on the future of space exploration. Earth will not last indefinitely, but space will. We need to colonize the universe as fast as physically and humanly possible. The universe is infinite - overpopulation is not possible, and humanity would have no single point of failure. If you don't believe in humanity living among the stars, you believe humanity should die out.

We have a mission. One that matters.

Our short-term mission is to ensure that humanity will begin to venture out into space and leave the small rock which it's currently confined to. By doing so, humanity can prosper in the depths of space, understand the universe, discover various forms of intelligent alien life, and much more. Our long-term goal is to ensure that humanity expands to as many places as possible in the cosmos. By doing so, humanity's continued existence would be ensured. We encourage the general public to pursue careers in STEM.

WE WANT YOU TO HELP ADVANCE OUR SPECIES

We want you to help push our species, humanity, forward, by getting involved in anything STEM, because that's the future. Right now humanity is messing around far too much. We operate unbelievably inefficiently and waste lots of time doing things that don't really matter. STEM is the future of humanity. Of the 1.8 million bachelor's degrees awarded in 2015–16, only about 331,000 (18%) were in STEM fields. That number is not nearly as high enough as it should be.

TECHNOLOGICAL PROGRESSION IS KEY

Technology is a key factor in human progress. Modernized cities are great examples of this, as they provide more efficient and sustainable ways to live and work. They also help businesses be more productive and efficient, as well as provide citizens with a safe and secure environment. Modernized cities can also attract investors, which leads to further economic development. In short, advances in technology are essential for humanity's progress, and modernized cities are a good example of this.

the dangers of not advancing humanity

Not working hard to advance technologically can have serious consequences for humanity. It can lead to a decline in our competitive edge and a decrease in our overall standard of living. A lack of technological progress can also have social impacts, such as conflict and instability. For example, if we do not keep up with the latest technologies and ideas, we may struggle to maintain a high standard of living and meet the needs of an increasing global population. It is important for humanity to continue working hard to advance technologically in order to avoid the dangers of stagnation and decline.
