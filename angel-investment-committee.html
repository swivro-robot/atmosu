investing in the future of human civilization

Atmos Angel Investments is a venture capital firm that specializes in providing funding for early-stage companies. Our unique approach to investing involves an investment council made up of 40 permanent council members who pool their resources together to invest in promising businesses.

Our council members are carefully selected primarily by the Atmos CEO based on their expertise and experience in various industries, as well as their overall intelligence, and more, giving us a diverse and well-rounded perspective on potential investments. Each council member would typically invest an equal amount of money into a company if all council members agree on the same investment terms before proceeding with the investment. If a permanent council member does not agree with the investment terms, they are free to choose not to invest in that specific company. But this will not stop the Atmos Angel Investment Committee from proceeding with the investment if the majority of council members agrees on the investment terms.

The only reason the majority of the council members have to agree with the investment in order for it to proceed is because, the less people there are willing to invest, the less capital available. So, if not enough people of the council are willing to invest, no meaningful amount of capital can be utilized for the investment.

Additionally, some members of the council can invest more money into a company than others, as long as the majority of the council agrees. The amount invested does not have to be the same for every member who chooses to invest.

As permanent council members of Atmos Angel Investments, we expect each member to make decisions based on factual information and logical reasoning. Given that council members are carefully selected by the CEO of Atmos, it is unlikely that there would often be a 50/50 tie in voting to invest or not invest in a business. Additionally, deliberations can take place for up to 48 hours to come to a final decision if initially not all council members agree.

At Atmos Angel Investments, we offer flexible investment terms that can fit the unique needs of each business we work with. Our ultimate goal is to provide not only financial aid, but also mentorship and guidance to help our portfolio companies thrive. We strive to establish mutually beneficial partnerships that benefit both our council members and the companies we invest in by generating profits for all parties involved.

Money is needed to live in this world, and that's why we need to generate profits, however, it's important to note that our goal is not to make as much money as possible (see our Success page), but to invest in companies and technologies that have the potential to make a meaningful impact on society. We are looking for companies that are working on technologies, products, or services that will make a large positive impact on humankind, improving people's lives in meaningful ways.

We believe that by supporting these companies financially, we can help to accelerate the pace of technological advancement and create a better future for all of humanity. Our investment council is, as previously mentioned, carefully selected based on their expertise in various industries, their understanding of the potential impact of technology on society, and more, allowing Atmos Angel Investments to make informed decisions about which companies to invest in and provide mentorship and guidance to help them succeed.

At Atmos Angel Investments, we are committed to creating mutually beneficial partnerships with companies that share our vision for a better future. We are dedicated to investing in the future of humanity, and we believe that our approach will ultimately aid in the advancement of technology and the improvement of people's lives. Before finalizing our investment in a company, we will monitor the company's operations for a given time period and will see how they operate on a day-to-day basis, and also how they respond to any advice or guidance we may try to provide.

council members carefully selected

Atmos Angel Investment is a venture capital firm that chooses its council members carefully. The council members are chosen by the CEO of Atmos and are not open for application. They are given the status of Temporary Council Member until the CEO feels that they are fit to be a Permanent Council Member. The goal is to have a council made up of intelligent members who can make decisions based on facts and not emotions. This will help Atmos invest in companies that are working on making positive technological changes for humanity. Ultimately, this will make the world (all of the places in the universe that humanity inhabits) a better place for everyone in numerous ways, whether it's health, environment, transportation, communication, etc.

council members keep investment profits

Atmos doesn't take any money from the profits made by council members who invested in a company. Even if the company they invested in becomes successful and doubles in value, and the council members make a huge profit, such as $500,000 each from a $20 million total investment (assuming all 40 members agreed to invest an equal amount, being $500k), Atmos doesn't get a cut. The council members get to keep all the money for themselves. We just encourage them to not only use the money for themselves but for the betterment of the world. The only way Atmos would benefit from such a successful investment is if the CEO of Atmos, who is also a council member, also makes a profit from the investment and uses the money for the Atmos Organization.
