CREATING A MODERN, efficient, and beautiful CITY ON the planet of MARS in the sol. system

PRESERVING the bright candle in a field of darkness that is HUMANITY STEP-BY-STEP, WE MUST begin WITH our lonely neighborly planet known as mars

Within your lifetime, you will be able to leave Earth and travel to Mars, whether if it's because you want to move there, you want to visit, you want to help develop Martian cities, etc. SpaceX, the world's most innovative aerospace entity, is currently working to build its Starship system which will enable life to become multiplanetary, by establishing multiple self-sustaining cities on Mars. The SpaceX Starship vehicle is capable of lifting over 100 tons to Mars. This high weight capacity allows for large amounts of heavy cargo to be transported from Earth to Mars, including food, equipment, water, machines to generate propellant on Mars (using Sabatier Process and mining robots), and much more. With multiple Starships from SpaceX, tens of thousands of metric tons can be transported to Mars simultaneously, to build numerous self-sustaining cities on the surface of the planet (and perhaps underground). Establishing a human presence on Mars will require overcoming a number of challenges, including the harsh environmental conditions on the planet and the logistical challenges of transporting people and supplies over such a vast distance. However, the potential benefits of establishing a human presence on Mars are significant, including the opportunity to learn more about the history and potential future of our own planet, the potential for new scientific discoveries, and the opportunity to expand the human sphere of influence beyond Earth.

Establishing a human presence on Mars has the potential to be a significant milestone for humanity and could have far-reaching implications for the future of our species. By establishing a self-sustaining city on Mars, humanity would be able to take its first steps towards becoming a multiplanetary species, capable of living and thriving on other planets and celestial bodies. This would be a major achievement and could lead to the expansion of human civilization beyond Earth, opening up new possibilities for exploration and development. In addition, establishing a human presence on Mars could also have numerous other practical benefits for humanity. For example, it could provide a backup plan in case of a catastrophic event on Earth, such as a global pandemic or a large-scale natural disaster. Having a human settlement on Mars would give humanity a way to ensure the survival of our species and could serve as a haven for those seeking to escape difficult circumstances on Earth.

YOU, YES YOU, CAN HELP US REACH MARS

Mars isn't that far away, compared to other known objects in the void of space. You can help us get there. You can help preserve humanity, and it's probably not as difficult as you might think. Please consider helping if you care about the future of humanity. Focus on things bigger than Earth politics and national borders, like occupying the cosmos. If we remain on Earth forever, we'll go extinct. If you help us expand to Mars, humanity would be immediately way less likely to go extinct after a self-sustaining city on Mars is established.

RETURNING TO EARTH'S CLOSE-BY MOON

We haven't gone back since 1972. It's time that we return to the Moon. If we are to colonize Mars, we should also be able to colonize the Moon. The more humans there are across the universe, the less likely we are to go extinct. For humanity, a city on the Moon may be almost as important as a city on Mars. We should expand as much and as far as we possibly can. That includes the Moon, Mars, and many more celestial bodies across the universe.

WORKING TOGETHER TO COLONIZE MARS

To show that humanity is able to successfully cooperate and work together towards a common goal, colonizing Mars is crucial. Mars colonization would involve the cooperation and coordination of countless distinctive institutions, organizations, and people. It would serve as an example for future cooperative projects of how we may overcome obstacles and collaborate to accomplish a common goal. It could also represent the potential of the human race.

MARS COULD SERVE AS A STEPPING STONE

Though Mars would be more than solely a stepping stone for humanity, another reason for colonizing Mars is that it could serve as a stepping stone for further exploration of the solar system and beyond. Mars is relatively close to Earth and has many characteristics that make it potentially habitable. If we can establish a human presence on Mars, it would open up many new possibilities for exploration and scientific discovery. We could use the planet as a base for further exploration of the solar system, and as a proving ground for new technologies and techniques. Colonizing Mars could be a major step forward in humanity's exploration of the universe.
