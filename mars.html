CREATING A MODERN CITY ON MARS

PRESERVING HUMANITY STEP-BY-STEP, WE MUST COLONIZE MARS TO LIVE ON, AND WE URGE YOU TO HELP.

Within your lifetime, you will be able to leave Earth and travel to Mars, whether if it's because you want to move there, you want to visit, you want to help develop Martian cities, etc. SpaceX, the world's most innovative private aerospace company, is currently working to build its Starship vehicle which should enable life to become multiplanetary, by establishing a self-sustaining city on Mars (Mars Base Alpha). The Starship vehicle is capable of lifting over 100 tons to Mars. This high weight capacity allows for large amounts of heavy cargo to be transported to Mars, including food, equipment, water, machines to generate propellant on Mars (using Sabatier Process and mining robots), and much more. With multiple Starships from SpaceX, tens of thousands of metric tons can be transported to and from Mars simultaneously, to build a self-sustaining city on the surface of the planet (and perhaps underground).

We mention SpaceX the most and have the most respect for them because they are the only private company (or entity of any kind) that is seriously focused on making humanity multiplanetary, starting with Mars. If another company does the same, we will mention them often as well. But we have not seen any other companies that have shown the same determination, seriousness, and innovation. SpaceX is shaping the future of humanity right now, and it's a fact regardless of your opinion or emotion. If you're not particularly the biggest fan of Elon Musk or SpaceX, just take a second to truly remove your bias, put away the emotional baggage for now, and take a close look at what SpaceX has done, is doing, and will do. This company could very well be the reason that humanity lives on.

YOU, YES YOU, CAN HELP US REACH MARS

Mars isn't that far away, compared to other known objects in the void of space. You can help us get there. You can help preserve humanity, and it's probably not as difficult as you might think. Please consider helping if you care about the future of humanity. Focus on things bigger than Earth politics and national borders, like occupying the cosmos. If we remain on Earth forever, we'll go extinct. If you help us expand to Mars, humanity would be immediately way less likely to go extinct after a self-sustaining city on Mars is established.

RETURNING TO EARTH'S CLOSE-BY MOON

We haven't gone back since 1972. It's time that we return to the Moon. If we are to colonize Mars, we should also be able to colonize the Moon. As we said on this page, the more humans there are across space, the less likely we are to go extinct. For humanity, a city on the Moon may be almost as important as a city on Mars. We should expand as much and as far as we possibly can. That includes the Moon, Mars, and many more celestial bodies across the universe.
