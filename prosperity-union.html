protecting humankind & its allies against hostile outside forces

in an infinite universe with infinite mass & energy, there are infinite possibilities. hostile alien forces exist somewhere infinitely close to us, and we need to protect ourselves.

Learn More humans on kardashev scale
There is a growing concern that the United Nations (UN) is not capable of protecting humanity from potential hostile forces outside our planet. Atmos aims to help unite humanity to defend ourselves against any potential threats from forces outside of humanity (ie: aliens). Given the vastness of the universe and the certainty of the existence of an evil alien empire, it is essential that we prepare ourselves to defend against possible attacks. This is why it is a terrible idea to intentionally send out radio signals into space, as we have no idea who might intercept them. It is possible that some super-advanced alien race could enslave us, and we have no defense against them. Our current nuclear warheads would be useless against aliens. We would be like a fly trying to ram into an elephant's side, hoping that the elephant even noticed.
If we, humanity, were to face a war against aliens, we would be like the modern US Military in 2023 fighting the Indus Valley Civilization over 4,000 years ago. The recent establishment of the Space Force by the United States was a positive step towards protecting humanity. However, it is crucial for the world to come together and create an alliance that would enable humanity to work together to defend ourselves from potential outside threats. We must be prepared for the worst-case scenario, and Atmos aims on helping us get prepared.

To ensure our survival, we must prioritize investing in technology and research that will allow us to detect and intercept, using new technologies developed by humanity, any hostile forces before they reach us, or have the ability to destroy them in complete war. This includes the development of advanced space-based detection systems, faster-than-light travel (warp drives utilizing negative energy density, proven to exist via the Casimir effect, and more), and powerful energy weapons. It is also essential to invest in education and awareness campaigns to prepare the public for the possibility of alien contact and provide them with the knowledge and skills to respond appropriately.
Moreover, we need to prioritize some degree of international cooperation and communication to enhance our chances of success. We must set aside political and ideological differences and work together towards a common goal of protecting humanity from potential threats. The more minds there are working on this defense technology, the more creative ideas we have, the faster we progress, and more.
The establishment of a unifying organization like Prosperity Union and the prioritization of advanced technology, education, and international cooperation are crucial steps towards protecting humanity from potential outside threats. It is imperative that we take proactive measures to prepare ourselves for the worst-case scenario, and we must work together to ensure our survival. By doing so, we can build a future where humanity can thrive and prosper, free from the fear of hostile forces beyond our sphere of influence.

The Planetary Union

MULTIPLE WORLDS ACROSS THE GALAXY UNITED

Humanity will without a doubt expand to other planets, even to planets beyond our solar system. Mars is first on the list, but we will likely inhabit hundreds of other planets in the future. There are countless opportunities for humanity if all of these planets, with their billions of inhabitants (as well as the billions of people living in plain outer space), unite. A galactic federation with the right policies can unite humanity.

SHARING KNOWLEDGE, RESORUCES, AND MORE

Creating a galactic union would allow for the sharing of knowledge, technology, and resources among member civilizations, potentially fostering innovation and advancement. This could enable member civilizations to pool their expertise and resources to create new technologies and capabilities, potentially leading to new insights and breakthroughs. The sharing of knowledge, technology, and resources within a galactic union could be a powerful force for progress and advancement.

CULTURES & IDEALS PRESERVED WHILE United

We understand that many cultures do not want their tradition to dissipate if humanity unites as one, and completely understand and agree with them. When humanity expands outwards into the universe and develops a galactic union, cultural ideals for each culture of humanity could be preserved, while all still working together to push humanity forward technologically. The future can't only be about hard work in STEM fields 24/7/365; some other unique, interesting, and exciting things must be involved and present.
