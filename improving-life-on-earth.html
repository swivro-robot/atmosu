LIFE ON EARTH MATTERS TOO

IMPROVING LIFE ON EARTH SHOULD BE ONE OF HUMANITY'S PRIMARY FOCUSES FOR THE FORESEEABLE FUTURE.

Unfortunately, as of 2022, life on Earth in most places is pretty bad, from a technological & political standpoint. Nations are threatening to use nuclear weapons to destroy each other, more global problems are being added to the already-large pile, most of our technology everywhere is outdated, and most cities don't look particularly modern. Yes, compared to hundreds of years ago, the world is extremely advanced and high-tech, however, we are capable of so much more. Humanity has so much potential, and we're hardly using any of it. We, humanity, need to learn that, when united, we are truly an amazing species that is capable of so many great things. But right now we're very divided, and aren't working together nearly as much as we should.

Look at the images below this paragraph section. That's what the average city on our beautiful planet, Earth, could look like if we unite and work together to push humanity forward. If we want a future that looks like that, we need to unite, and if you're someone who is helping to further divide humanity, you need to change. We can't fly into the future to see what's happened with humanity, but, hopefully we will learn to work together, and if we do, we will advance much faster, and it would make Earth a much better place to live, for everybody.
